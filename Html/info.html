About Us

 

Find Nearby Places is a student-friendly web platform developed to help individuals, especially freshers, easily discover essential locations around their colleges or hostels. Moving to a new city can be overwhelming, especially when students are not familiar with their surroundings.

This system provides quick access to nearby hostels, restaurants, hospitals, ATMs, libraries, and other important spots that are often needed in day-to-day life. The purpose is to make navigation simple and reduce the time spent searching for basic facilities.

The platform is built using modern web technologies such as HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript, ensuring a clean and responsive interface that works seamlessly across devices. It integrates the Google Maps API and Places API to provide real-time search results with accurate locations, directions, and details. Users can either type their queries or choose from predefined categories, and the system displays results within a default radius of 10 kilometers.

Users can also expand this range based on their preference. This makes it ideal for students, travelers, and newcomers who want quick and reliable information about their surroundings.
